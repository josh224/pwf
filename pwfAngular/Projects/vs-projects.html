Project Notification Pages

The projects listed below are all those across the Practice for which a project notification has been completed. To complete a new initial brief and review, or revise an existing, simply select by 'left clicking' with the cursor over the project in question - this initiates the initial brief and review process. To narrow down the projects showing, for example by cost centre, use the search and/ or filter facility; clicking on a column header will sort the data on the selected column, click the column header again to reverse the sort order.
Where the project you require is not included in the list, refer to the project partner/team leader and a) ensure that a project notification has been completed, or b) check that the project status hasn't been changed (on Nexus) to 'complete'. The 'Initial Brief and Review' should be completed as a minimum, by the Project Leader and Project Partner/Team Leader, however, it may include other members of the Internal Project Team such as the lead mechanical and electrical engineers, and project reviewers.
